3. Collection and Processing of Personal Data

We only collect and process personal data in accordance with legal requirements. This includes:

4. Purpose of Data Usage

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the platform
  • Improving and analyzing our services
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Your data is processed based on:

  • Your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), if required
  • The necessity to fulfill a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), such as ensuring security

6. Data Storage and Deletion

We store your personal data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by law.

7. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We only share your data if:

  • It is necessary to provide the service
  • We are legally required to do so
  • It is needed to enforce our rights

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access to your data (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Correction of inaccurate data (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Deletion of your data (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Objection to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

To exercise your rights, contact us using the provided contact details.

9. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

10. Changes to the Privacy Policy
